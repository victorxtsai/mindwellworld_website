Privacy Policy

Introduction

This privacy policy explains how we, Mindwell World ("Mindwell", "our", "us" or "we"), collect, use, and protect your personal data when you use our websites, products, applications, and other Mindwell services (our "Service").

1. Information We Collect

1.1. Information You Provide To Us

When you use our Service or communicate with us, you provide certain personal information. This may include:

  • Account Data: When you create or use a Mindwell World account to access our Service, we may collect your contact information (e.g., name, email address), account credentials, and other information you provide during registration. If you register or log into our Service via a third party (such as Google or Apple), that service may send certain account information to us.
  • Payment Data: When you make transactions through our Service, we may collect payment and transaction information, such as billing address and payment method details.
  • Contact and Communications Data: When you communicate with us, we may collect contact information (e.g., name, email address) and any additional information you voluntarily provide in your communications with us.
  • User Content Data: When you use our Service, we may collect personal information included in your inputs, such as your daily journal entries, chatbot interactions, uploaded media (e.g., photos or audio clips), and mood ratings.
  • Feedback Data: When you provide feedback, suggestions, or other comments about our Service (e.g., surveys, emails, in-app forms, or social media), we may collect and store the information you share. This may include details about your experience, opinions on new features, or comments on how we can improve.
  • Applicant Data: When you apply for a job with us, we may collect your personal information such as your contact details, resume/CV, education and work history, and visa status.

1.2. Information Automatically Provided To Us

When you use or interact with our Service, your device typically provides us with certain information automatically. We use this data to understand how our Service is being used, improve performance, and enhance your experience. This information may be collected through technologies such as cookies, web beacons, local storage, and clear GIFs. The information may include:

  • Device Data: Technical information including your device type, browser type and version, operating system, application version, device features.
  • Usage Data: Information about your use of the Service such as pages you view, date and time of your visit, duration of visits, pages visited, browsing history, search history, traffic data, standard server log information, and other data related to your interaction with the Service.
  • Inferred Location Data: IP address information, which includes country-level, state-level, and city-level location.

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1. General Ways We Use Your Information

In general, we use your data in the following ways:

  • To provide, maintain, develop, and improve our Service: We use your data to enhance our features, troubleshoot issues, conduct research, and ensure our offerings remain helpful and relevant.
  • To make our Service more relevant and personalized for you: We use your data to identify trends and tailor recommendations, insights, and content that best match your interests or goals.
  • To comply with our legal obligations: We may process your data to meet regulatory and compliance requirements (e.g., tax, audit, anti-fraud), cooperate with law enforcement, or fulfill other legal duties.
  • To ensure the security and integrity of our Service: We use your data to protect against fraudulent activity, enforce our terms, and safeguard user accounts and information.
  • To provide support and assistance in relation to our Service: We use your data to respond to your requests, resolve technical issues, and offer user support.
  • To process payments for our Service: We use your payment details and related information to complete transactions, manage subscriptions, and handle refunds if necessary.
  • To process job applications: We use applicant data to evaluate qualifications, communicate with candidates, and facilitate our hiring process.

2.2. Data-Specific Ways Information Is Used

The data we collect is used in the following ways:

  • Account Data: We use your account information (e.g., name, email, login credentials) to create, authenticate, and manage your account, as well as to communicate with you about updates, security alerts, and important notices.
  • Payment Data: We use your payment and transaction information to process purchases, verify billing details, and fulfill financial obligations (e.g., invoices, refunds, or subscription management). We may also use this data for fraud detection and to meet applicable legal or regulatory requirements.
  • User Content Data: We use the content you voluntarily provide—such as daily journal entries, chatbot messages, uploaded media, and mood logs—to personalize your experience, deliver insights or recommendations, and help you track your progress over time. We also use this data to refine features and improve the quality of our Service.
  • Feedback Data: We use feedback, suggestions, and comments you provide to understand user needs and preferences, resolve issues, and guide future improvements or new features. In some cases, we may contact you for clarification on your feedback.
  • Applicant Data: We use your resume/CV, contact details, and other job-related information to evaluate your qualifications, communicate with you regarding your application, and maintain records related to our recruitment activities.
  • Device Data: We use information such as device type, operating system, app version, and unique device identifiers to optimize our Service for different platforms, diagnose technical issues, enhance compatibility, and plan future feature developments.
  • Usage Data: We analyze how you interact with our Service—pages visited, features used, and time spent—to enhance user experience, identify popular or underused features, and guide iterative improvements. This helps us tailor the Service to align with your needs and preferences.
  • Inferred Location Data: We use approximate location information (e.g., derived from your IP address) to personalize certain features and content (such as local recommendations or language settings). This also helps us detect and protect against fraudulent or unauthorized use.

2.3. We Will Not Use Your Data in the Following Ways:

  • We will not sell your data to third parties.
  • We will not provide any deanonymized or re-identified data to third parties without your consent, unless required by law.

4. Security Of Your Information

4.1. Storing Your Data Locally

Data including your journal entries are currently stored unencrypted on your local device. Because of this, please treat your device like a physical journal, i.e. do not misplace it or carelessly let others use it. For registered users, while a user cannot access your data through the Service itself without logging in, someone skilled may still be able to access the unencrypted information using third party tools.

4.1. Transmitting Your Data Servers

If and when data is sent to our servers for storage, it is encrypted first locally before transmission. This means that only encrypted data is transmitted, preventing third parties from intercepting the data and viewing it.

4.2. Storing Your Data On The Cloud

Data including your journal entries are always stored encrypted on the servers. From time to time, we may unencrypt the data to perform analytics and use them in ways defined in section 2.

5. Sharing Of Your Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties, other than as follows:

  • With our contracted vendors, service providers and consultants: To assist in providing the Service to you or performing business operations, we provide your personal information to vendors and service providers including providers of hosting services, cloud services, web analytics providers, content delivery services, and payment and transaction providers and other technology service providers, for the purposes described above.
  • In connection with business transfers: In connection with or during negotiation of any merger, financing, acquisition, bankruptcy, dissolution, transaction, or proceeding involving sale, transfer, divestiture, or disclosure of all or a portion of our business or assets to another company. If required by applicable laws, we will use reasonable efforts to notify you of any transfer of personal information to an unaffiliated third party.
  • To comply with our legal obligations: To (i) comply with laws or to respond to lawful requests and legal process, (ii) protect the rights and property of Mindwell and our agents, customers, and others, including to enforce our agreements, policies, and terms of use, or (iii) in an emergency, to protect the personal safety of Mindwell, its customers, or any person.
With our related companies: We may disclose personal information to our related companies to the extent such sharing is necessary to fulfill a request you have submitted via our Service or for customer management, customer support, technical operations, or the purposes described above. We may also create and share anonymized, aggregated and de-identified usage data with our related companies and also with third parties to help us perform analysis and make improvements. Additionally, we may share anonymous usage data on an aggregate basis in the normal course of operating our business. We may share information publicly to show trends in the use of our Service, for example, show how many users have downloaded our service.

6. Your Rights

Depending on applicable data protection legislation, under certain circumstances, you may have the following rights concerning your personal data:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request that we correct or update any inaccuracies regarding your personal data.
  • Request that we delete your personal data.
  • Request that we restrict the processing of your personal data.

Data Deletion Request

If you would like to delete your data associated with your account, please contact us at support@mindwell.io.

7. Changes To This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. Where appropriate, we may notify you of any significant changes via the email address you provided or a prominent notice on our website. Please review this Privacy Policy regularly.
