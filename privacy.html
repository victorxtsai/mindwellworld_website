Privacy Policy

Introduction

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we, Mindwell World ("Mindwell", "our", "us" or "we"), collect, use, and protect your personal data when you use our websites, products, applications, and other Mindwell services (our "Service").

Given the nature of the data you provide and our overall mission, Mindwell believes in demonstrating ethical leadership when it comes to managing your data. This means we will not sell your data to third parties.

1. Information We Collect

1.1. Information You Provide

When you use our Service or communicate with us, you provide certain personal information. This may include:

  • Account Data: When a Mindwell World account is used to access the Service, we will collect your contact information, account credentials, and other relevant information. If you register or log into our Service using another service, that service will send your information to us.
  • Payment Data: We will collect payment and transaction information (such as payment card information) directly from you when you make a transaction with us.
  • Contact and Communications Data: If you communicate with us, we will collect contact information such as your name, email address, any other contact details you voluntarily choose to provide us, and any additional information you provide in your communications with us.
  • User Content: When you use our Service, we collect personal information that is included in user inputs to the Service (like Journal and Mood information in our Daily Reflections product).
  • Feedback Data: Where applicable, we will collect your feedback.
  • Applicant Data: If you apply for a job with us, we will collect your personal information such as your contact details, resume/CV, education and work history, and visa status. You also may choose to provide your gender, ethnicity, veteran status, disability status, and links to your website, blog, portfolio, LinkedIn or other public profiles.

1.2. Information Automatically Provided To Us

When you use or interact with our Service, your device typically automatically provides us with certain device and usage information. This may include:

  • Device Data: Technical information such as your IP address, device type, country from which you access, browser type and version, operating system, device features, etc...
  • Usage Data: Information about your use of the Service, pages you view, date and time of your visit, duration of visits, pages visited, browsing history, search history, traffic data, standard server log information, and other data related to your interaction with the Service.
  • Inferred Location Data: Your country-level, state-level, and city-level location based on your device's IP address.

This information may also be provided to us through methods of web analysis, including with the help of technologies, such as cookies, web beacons, and clear gifs.
